### 1. Waermepumpen (Heat Pumps)
**URL:** `/de/products/waermepumpen`
**Image:** `/sites/default/files/inline-images/AdobeStock_513066134_1.jpeg`

**Introduction:** "Innovative Systeme, die Effizienz, Umweltfreundlichkeit und einfache Bedienung kombinieren" for heating and cooling. Company offers various established heat pump types from multiple brands.

**Heat Pump Types:**

**Luft-Wasser-Waermepumpen (Air-Water Heat Pumps):**
- Use air as energy source
- Cost-effective and simple installation
- Work efficiently at various outdoor temperatures
- Suitable for buildings needing versatile heating/cooling

**Wasser-Wasser-Waermepumpen (Water-Water Heat Pumps):**
- Use underground water sources
- Extremely efficient
- Maintain stable indoor temperatures at low outdoor temperatures
- Ideal for continuous heating/cooling applications

**Erdreich-Wasser-Waermepumpen (Ground-Source Heat Pumps):**
- Extract heat from earth
- Reliable and cost-effective
- Provide constant, stable temperatures
- Work in homes and commercial buildings

### 4. Solarwarmwasserbereiter (Solar Hot Water Heaters)

**Flachkollektoren (Flat Plate Collectors):**

- "Stellen somit mehr Energie fuer die Warmwasserbereitung und fuer die Heizung zur Verfuegung"
- Optimal for cold climates
- Image: `/sites/default/files/inline-images/AdobeStock_487559382_0.jpeg`

**Key Benefits:**

- **Umweltfreundlichkeit:** Zero emissions; environmental protection
